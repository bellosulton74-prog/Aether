About Aether

Aether is redefining how NFTs are launched starting on Solana, then expanding multi-chain.

We’re building a next-generation launchpad where anyone can launch an NFT collection in minutes, at minimal cost and across any chain Solana, Ethereum, Base, Hyperliquid, and beyond. ( Mainly focusing on Solana firstly ) But Aether isn’t just about launching NFTs, it’s about rewarding the ecosystem that powers them. All fees from project launches flow into a community treasury, redistributed monthly to holders based on tiers. Even a single Aether NFT earns a tangible share of the ecosystem’s growth.

Holders also unlock:
  • A straight up monthly revenue share from the dao treasury
  • Early access & whitelists from top upcoming projects.
  • Priority placement and visibility for collections launched through Aether.
  • Tier-based multipliers that enhance your share of treasury rewards and influence in platform governance.
  • And as Aether expands multichain, cross-chain launch rewards and treasury bonuses designed to grow with every integrated network.
Aether is more than a launchpad, it’s the portal of creation, wealth, and opportunity for the next era of decentralized NFT ecosystems.
